# 📊 Power BI Dashboard – Wealth & Insurance Business Analytics

## 🔍 Overview
An executive Power BI dashboard providing a 360-degree view 
of Wealth and Insurance business performance with YoY analysis 
and advisor-level insights.

## ✨ Features
### Executive Summary
- CY Sales, Withdrawals & Net Sales KPIs
- Sales by Business Line (Donut Chart)
- Sales by Risk Level (Bar + Line Chart)
- Sales by Region (Map Visual)
- Sales by Product comparison (CY vs PY)

### Wealth Page
- Mutual Fund, Retirement & Stock Portfolio KPIs
- Breakdown by Date (Line Chart)
- Breakdown by Contract Type
- Breakdown by Region (Treemap)
- Advisor Performance Details

### Insurance Page
- Car, Home & Life Insurance KPIs with YoY
- Performance Over Date (2023 vs 2024)
- Sales by Risk Level
- Advisor Details Table

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- DAX (YoY, KPI Measures)
- Power Query (Data Transformation)
- Data Modeling (Fact & Dimension Tables)
- Map Visual, Treemap, Donut, Bar Charts
